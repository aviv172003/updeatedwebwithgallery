mashroom pasta recipe:

ingredients:

  • 1 pack of pasta
  • 250ml heavy cream
  • 2 packs of mashroom
  • 1 glass of milk
  • 1 small choped onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • salt & pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 1 spoon of soup powder
  • basil
  • 30grams of butter

prepretion:

step 1:
cook the pasta in a pot with salt and boiling water untill almost ready
save 1\4 of a cup of the pasts water for later

step 2:
in a pan melt the butter and fry the onion until it's a litle bit golden

step 3:
add the mashroom and wait until the pan fills with the mashroom's liquid

step 4:
add heavy cream milk and 1\4 cup of pasta water add spices for taste

step 5:
stir until thickend take of the heat and add the pasta
